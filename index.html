About LIB

Liban and Iman Pharmaceutical Wholesaler, was first established in 1991 under the vision of Dr Mohammad Liban in Berbera to raise the standards of medicine in Somalia.

Today we are a worldwide registered, respected and well known company with over 25 year plus history working hard on the same vision of importing,marketing and distributing the highest quality of pharmaceutical medicines to Africa with great customer service.
We are now based in multiple locations in Somalia including Xamar, Kismayo, Burco,Berbera, Hargeisa, Wajale and Borama, we have over 100 staff members that work together with the same level of commitment, dedication and passion in raising the quality standards of all medicines.

Given our commitment to training, teamwork and working with excellence we are confident that the number of professionally qualified staff will continue to rise and will continue to only do bigger things.
